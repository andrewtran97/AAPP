
AAPP Claim Boundaries

AAPP / Agent Black Box is an AI Agent Control + Evidence Plane.

It helps developers inspect agent and CI/CD action surfaces, bind actions to deterministic policy paths, record evidence, verify evidence integrity, govern evidence exposure, and produce incident-ready casefile artifacts.

Evidence Boundary

AAPP evidence artifacts support review.

Tamper-evident means evidence changes can be detected inside the designed evidence path. It does not mean external storage or operating environments cannot alter files.

Data Boundary

AAPP must not claim ownership of customer data.

Training, analytics, export, and retention require explicit rights boundaries in later phases.

No rights means no training use.

Scope Boundary

AAPP examples are local developer examples.

Active scanning requires scope.

Third-party targets require written scope.

Runtime Boundary
